Don't use standard "Q1" format. Use Valve220, which will always get the correct texture settings.

In Q1 format, .map textures are projected along one of 3 world axes:
If a face is at 45 degrees:
*	The Y-axis takes precedent over X-axis.
*	The Z-axis takes precedent over Y-axis and X-axis.
In Valve220 format, we still need to pick an axis to ensure that we can get valid texture axes that are perpendicular to the face normal.
